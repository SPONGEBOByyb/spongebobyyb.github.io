Day9 my favorite teacher

Mrs. Davis, my history teacher, was someone who made the past feel alive. She had a gift for storytelling, and instead of just listing dates and events, she would paint vivid pictures of historical figures and their lives. Her passion for history was contagious, and she encouraged us to think critically and ask questions rather than just memorize facts.

  • alive 鲜活的，有生命的
  • have a gift for sth 有…的天赋
  • storytelling n. 讲故事
  • list sth 列出…
  • vivid 生动的，鲜明的
  • figure 人物
  • passion 热情
  • contagious 有感染力的
  • think critically 批判性地思考
  • memorize sth 记住…
  • fact 事实

Day10 特殊的照片

I love a photo of my son building his first snowman in our backyard on a snowy winter day. His cheeks are rosy, and he has a big smile on his face as he carefully places each bit of snow together. His excitement is so contagious; you can almost feel his happiness just by looking at the photo. Every time I see it, I’m reminded of how magical childhood is and how joyful the simplest things can be.

很喜欢一张我儿子在后院第一次堆雪人的照片，那是一个飘雪的冬日。照片中他脸颊红扑扑的，笑得很开心，小心翼翼地把雪堆在一起。他的兴奋特别有感染力，看着照片几乎能感受到他的快乐。每次看到这张照片，我都想起童年的神奇和简单事物带来的快乐。

  • cheek 面颊
  • rosy 红润的
  • place v.放置
  • excitement n.兴奋
  • contagious 有感染力的
  • remind sb of sth 使某人想起某事
  • magical 神奇的
  • joyful 快乐的
  • simple 简单的

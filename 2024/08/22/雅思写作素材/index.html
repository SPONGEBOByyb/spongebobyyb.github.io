2. 写作总结

bar chart 柱状图

pie chart 饼图

line graph 线图

table 表格图

diagram 地图或者流程

C19 Test4 Task1

  • The pie chart gives information about …
  • the bar chart shows …
  • the amount of …
  • the proportion of … / the percentage of …
  • considerably
    • Overall, the amount of young people under 11 attending dance classes is considerably larger for those under 11 than for the age group 11 to 16.
  • 比较级 + than any other …
  • significantly
    • The proportion of dance classes located in private studios is significantly larger than any other location.
  • It is obvious that …
  • the figure
  • at … and … respectively / with … and … respectively
  • (around +数字)
  • slightly
    • the amount under 11 is slightly larger than the figure between 11 and 16, with around 450 and 425 respectively.

Task2

经典25题串讲

雅思写作经典大作文25题串讲，搞定25篇雅思大作文，雅思写作不再怕_哔哩哔哩_bilibili

开头段

  • some people believe that …

  • It is true that …

  • Personally, I believe that …

    • I therefore disagree with …

  • have different views about …

  • there are some benefits to do sth

  • I would argue that … 我认为。。。

  • It is no doubt true that …

  • have differing views with regard to … 对……有不同的看法

  • In my view, … 在我看来， （用于第一段预告观点）

核心段

  • On the one hand,… On the other hand, …
  • It is possible to …
  • Firstly, … Secondly, … Finally, …
  • For example, …
  • In my opinion, …
  • In my case, … 就我而言
  • Despite …
  • In this way, …‘
  • it is better to …
  • The option to …
  • be attractive for several reasons

结尾段

  • In conclusion, …
  • It is true that …
  • I believe that …
    • In conclusion, …, but … for different reasons.
  • In conclusion, it seems to me that … 在我看来。。。
  • so that …
  • at the same time
  • For the reasons mentioned above, …

C19 Test4 Task1

  • The pie chart gives information about …
  • the bar chart shows …
  • the amount of …
  • the proportion of … / the percentage of …
  • considerably
    • Overall, the amount of young people under 11 attending dance classes is considerably larger for those under 11 than for the age group 11 to 16.
  • 比较级 + than any other …
  • significantly
    • The proportion of dance classes located in private studios is significantly larger than any other location.
  • It is obvious that …
  • the figure
  • at … and … respectively / with … and … respectively
  • (around +数字)
  • slightly
    • the amount under 11 is slightly larger than the figure between 11 and 16, with around 450 and 425 respectively.

C18 T2 Task2(Discuss)

  • 开头：
    • people have different views about …
    • In my view, …
    • in addition to 除了……之外：表示在已经提到的事物之外，还有额外的事物或观点。
  • 核心段1
    • some people believe that …
    • The reasoning behind this argument is that …
    • secure an excellent job 找到一份好工作
    • in turn 反过来
    • improve their quality of life 提升他们的生活质量
  • 核心段2
    • There are several main reasons why …
    • rather than 而不是（可以用作转折，加doing）
      • rather than limiting their chances to only one major
  • 结尾段
    • alongside 与
    • Personally, I strongly believe that … . Although … , … . Therefore, my view is that … .

Nowadays some people have anti-social behavior and lack respect to others. What are the reasons? What are the solutions?

  • 开头

    • It is true that + 现象
    • There could be several reasons why this is the case and I believe governments should implement solutions to this problem.

  • 核心段（原因分析）

    • There are several reasons why + 现象
    • contemporary population growth
      • 可以使用“comtemporary+现象”来代替nowadays
    • a lack of sufficient educational resources 缺乏足够的教育资源
    • in society 在社会中：指一个人在社会中的地位、角色或行为
    • acquire a complete education 接受完整的教育
    • be prone to doing 易于；倾向于
    • the cost of living = living cost 生活成本

  • 核心段（解决方法）

    • On the one hand, … . On the other hand, … .

    • introduce some rules and regulations 出台一些规章制度

    • impose punishments and fines 施以惩罚和罚款

    • reflect on 仔细思考某事，并给予应有的考虑

    • the consequences of …的后果

    • in a friendly manner 以友好的方式

    • address this issue 处理这个问题

  • 结尾段

    • implement certain penalties 实施特定的惩罚
    • receive a quality education 接收一个优质教育
    • respect others / respect for others

Some people that have been in prison become good citizens later. Some people think that they are the best people to talk to school students about the dangers of committing crime. To what extent do you agree or disagree?

  • It is sometimes argued that …
  • ex-prisoners 前囚犯
  • the dangers of committing crime 犯罪的危险
  • I completely agree with this idea 我完全同意这个观点
  • real-life insights and personal experiences 真实的视角和个人经历
  • strongly resonate with 与…产生强烈的共鸣
  • the detrimental effects of crime 犯罪的有害影响
  • be profoundly impactful on … 对… 产生深远的影响
  • deter sb from doing sth 阻止某人做某事
  • engage in 从事
    • engaging 引人入胜的
  • their firsthand accounts of … 对于…的第一手描述
  • imprisonment and subsequent reformation 监禁和随后的改造
  • illustrating the serious consequences 说明严重后果
  • serve as exemplars (of) 作为…的榜样
  • surmount previous errors 克服之前的错误
  • lead successful, law-abiding lives 过上成功、守法的生活
    • lead a normal life 过上正常的生活
  • rehabilitation and redemption 改过自新
  • inspire sb to do sth 鼓励某人做某事
  • outcasts 被抛弃的人
  • In conclusion, I believe that …
  • in a way, 在某种程度上
  • a role model for sb 某人的榜样

逻辑

Nowadays some people have anti-social behavior and lack respect to others. What are the reasons? What are the solutions?

  • ・ Point 1: Poor upbringing and lack of parental guidance can lead to anti-social behavior and disrespect towards others.
  • ・ Example: Without proper moral and ethical education at home, children may grow up without understanding the importance of respecting others, leading to anti-social tendencies in adulthood.
  • ・ 素材 1：不良的家庭教育和缺乏父母的指导会导致反社会行为和对他人的不尊重。
  • ・ 例子：如果在家里没有得到正确的道德和伦理教育，孩子可能在成长过程中不了解尊重他人的重要性，从而导致成年后的反社会倾向。
  • ・ Point 2: Media and internet influence can perpetuate negative behaviors and lack of respect for others.
  • ・ Example: Exposure to violent or disrespectful content on television or the internet can normalize such behaviors, especially for impressionable young people.
  • ・ 素材 2：媒体和互联网的影响可能会延续负面行为以及对他人的不尊重。
  • ・ 例子：接触影视或网络上的暴力或不尊重内容，尤其对易受影响的年轻人来说，这些行为可能会被正常化。
  • ・ Point 3: Stressful and competitive environments, such as workplaces or schools, can contribute to anti-social behavior.
  • ・ Example: High levels of stress and pressure can lead individuals to act out negatively as a coping mechanism, resulting in disrespectful behavior toward others.
  • ・ 素材 3：工作环境或学校等充满压力和竞争的环境会助长反社会行为。
  • ・ 例子：高压环境中，人们可能会通过消极行为作为应对机制，从而导致对他人的不尊重行为。
  • ・ Point 4: Socio-economic factors, such as poverty and unemployment, can lead to frustration and anti-social behavior.
  • ・ Example: Persistent financial difficulties can create a sense of hopelessness and frustration, which may manifest as disrespect and anti-social actions towards others.
  • ・ 素材 4：贫困和失业等社会经济因素会导致挫败感和反社会行为。
  • ・ 例子：持续的经济困难会产生无望感和挫败感，从而表现为对他人的不尊重和反社会行为。
  • ・ Point 5: Implementing educational programs focused on empathy and social skills can mitigate anti-social behavior.
  • ・ Example: Schools and community programs that teach empathy, collaboration, and social etiquette can help individuals understand the importance of respectful interactions.
  • ・ 素材 5：实施以同理心和社交技能为重点的教育项目可以减轻反社会行为。
  • ・ 例子：学校和社区项目教授同理心、合作和社交礼仪，可以帮助个人理解尊重互动的重要性。

Some people that have been in prison become good citizens later. Some people think that they are the best people to talk to school students about the dangers of committing crime. To what extent do you agree or disagree?

  • ・ Point 1: Ex-prisoners who have reformed can provide real-life insights and personal experiences that resonate strongly with young students.
  • ・ Example: Their firsthand accounts of imprisonment and subsequent reformation can deter students from engaging in criminal activities by illustrating the serious consequences.
  • ・ 素材 1： 曾经改过自新的前囚犯可以提供与学生强烈共鸣的真实生活见解和个人经历。
  • ・ 例子：他们关于入狱和随后改过自新的第一手描述可以通过展示严重后果来阻止学生从事犯罪活动。
  • ・ Point 2: Ex-offenders can serve as powerful role models of change, showing students that people can overcome past mistakes and lead successful, law-abiding lives.
  • ・ Example: By sharing their stories of rehabilitation and redemption, they can inspire students to make positive life choices and avoid crime.
  • ・ 素材 2： 前罪犯可以作为改变的强大榜样，向学生表明人们可以克服过去的错误，过上成功、守法的生活。
  • ・ 例子：通过分享他们康复和救赎的故事，他们可以激励学生做出积极的生活选择，避免犯罪。
  • ・ Point 3: Listening to reformed ex-criminals can humanize the consequences of crime, making the risks more tangible and impactful for students.
  • ・ Example: Students may better understand the far-reaching effects of crime on individuals, families, and communities through poignant and empathetic dialogues with these speakers.
  • ・ 素材 3： 聆听改过自新的前罪犯可以使犯罪的后果变得更加人性化，使学生更能感受到风险的切实性和影响力。
  • ・ 例子：学生可能通过与这些讲者感人的、充满同情的对话，更好地了解犯罪对个人、家庭和社区的深远影响。
  • ・ Point 4: Utilizing ex-prisoners as speakers can foster a sense of social responsibility and rehabilitation, promoting the idea that society should support and reintegrate former offenders.
  • ・ Example: It encourages students to view ex-criminals not merely as outcasts, but as individuals who are capable of positive change and deserve a second chance.
  • ・ 素材 4： 利用前囚犯作为演讲者可以培养社会责任感和康复意识，推广社会应支持和重新融入前罪犯的理念。
  • ・ 例子：这鼓励学生不仅将前罪犯视为被遗弃者，还将他们视为有能力做出积极改变并应得第二次机会的个人。
  • ・ Point 5: Critics argue that ex-prisoners may not always be suitable due to potential glorification of their past or possible relapse into criminal behavior.
  • ・ Example: Some believe other approaches, such as professional educators or law enforcement officers delivering crime prevention talks, may be more appropriate and effective.
  • ・ 素材 5： 批评者认为前囚犯不总是适合的，因为他们过去的行为可能被美化，或者可能再次陷入犯罪行为。
  • ・ 例子：一些人认为其他方法，如由专业教育工作者或执法人员进行预防犯罪讲座，可能更适当和有效。

杨帅雅思口语

第三节课

第一部分 Part3练习

1. What can we do to combat traffic congestion?

I believe there are various things governments and individuals can do to alleviate traffic congestion, and one of them is a congestion charge. When a driver has to pay to drive into the city center, they will think twice and might turn to public transportation. London is a good example of this practice. A congestion charge has been in place for many years and it has been very successful in reducing traffic congestion.

  • traffic jam 可数 ；traffic congestion 不可数，表示一个现象，问题

  • navigation apps; google maps; apple maps

    • google maps is good

  • sitting in a jam

  • combat 战斗，战役；解决

    • solve a problem
    • resolve a problem
    • tackle a problem
    • combat a problem

  • various

    • a variety of
    • a wided range of
    • tons of
    • wide

  • it depends on the person; it depends on the individual

  • air pollution

    • air quality
    • on most days

  • practice 行为，实践

    • put sth into practice
    • bear fruit
What words and phrases have you learned? 略

2. What’s the most popular form of physical activity in your country?

I think tai chi is by far the most popular form of exercise in our country, especially among senior citizens. But it has also been gaining popularity among younger generations. This traditional martial art involves slow, gentle movements, deep breathing and relaxation and has various benefits. For example, studies have shown that practicing tai chi regularly can slow the development of Parkinson’s. Plus, some aspects of tai chi can also help with people’s memory and concentration.

  • meditation 冥想
  • take up a form of exercise
  • and reap a lot of benefits
  • practice tai chi
  • form of exercise
    • form of transportation
    • form of relaxation
  • by far 最…的，比第二名厉害很多
  • gain popularity = become more and more popular
    • yoga has been gaining popularity in China
    • pilates has been …
    • meditation has been …
  • martial art
    • kungfu
  • reading is a good form of relaxation

  • live a healthy life/lifestyle

no, not really. I’ve been learning English for over 10 years now, and I’m still far from good at it; learning English is sooooooo challenging. 第三部分 练习

1. How important are friends to you?

There’s no doubt that friends are extremely important to me because when I’m going through a difficult time, they’re always there, they all show up for me. They check in and listen carefully to how I’m feeling at the moment.

Actually, just last month, my girlfriend broke up with me, and my heart was broken; I was devastated. Afew of my friends came over to my apartment with home cooked food, and we just talked and talked and talked. They weren’t trying to fix the problem; They just sat in the mud with me, but that made me feel so much better.

  • give a hoot about sth 嘲笑

2. Do you like to stay in a place with a lot of noise?

Definitely not. Nobody likes to be in a noisy place because it makes up stressed, and messes with out health.

For instance, there was a lot of construction going on in my neighborhood a couple of weeks ago, and unfortunately, I had to stay home all day for online classes. The noises was non-stop, and I guess because of that, I developed a very bad headache and perhaps my cold was also linked to that because my immunity was down. (回顾过去，为了证明观点)

3. When do you usually take public transportation, in your everyday life or when you are travelling? （主动添加过去，进行对比）

Well, I only take public transportation when I’m visiting a differrent city or country. In my daily life, I drive no matter where I go-to work, to go shopping, to visit friends and so on, whereas back in the day, I mean, just 5 years ago, public transportation was my go-to, particularly the subway. It’s both convenient and reliable, so I took that every day. My life has changed a lot.

  • back in the day 在曾经

4. Please describe your hometown a little.

Well, um, off the top of my head, I want to talk about how clean it is in my hometown air quality is really good now, whereas back in the day, I mean ,over 10 years ago, It was really smoggy on many days of the year. pollution levels were quite high before. I think my hometown has changed a lot.

  • smoggy = smoke + fog 雾霾的

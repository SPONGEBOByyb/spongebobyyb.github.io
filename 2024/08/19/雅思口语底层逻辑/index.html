雅思口语底层逻辑（小红书）

1. 口语开场废话

  • 重复题目
    • Speaking of …
    • When it comes to …
    • Regarding/Concerning …
  • 引出话题
    • What comes/floats into my mind first is …
    • The first thing that springs to my head is
    • off the top of my head

2. Something不会

  • 原因
    • beyond my grasp
    • be clumsy in
    • aptitude
  • 事物本身
    • complicated
    • inaccessible
    • obscure
    • elusive
  • difficult 替换为 demanding
  • 感受
    • overwhelmed and inadequate

3. 替换know, understand, important, really

  • know = be clued up about
    • I know how to do this
    • I am clued up about how to do this
  • understand = grasp
    • 例一
      • I can understand the meaning of your words
      • I can grasp the meaning of your words
    • 例二
      • I don’t understand what you mean
      • I don’t grasp what you mean
  • important = indispensable
    • 例一
      • English is important in today’s society
      • English is indispensable in today’s society
    • 例二
      • Money is important
      • Money is indispensable
  • really = genuinely
    • 例一
      • I’m really interested in this book
      • I’m genuinely interested in this book
    • 例二
      • I really like your style
      • I genuinely like your style

4. 底层逻辑：broaden my horizons(看)

  • have access to
  • digest whatever aspects I can turn into benefit
  • good way to expand my boundary of knowledge and experience
  • have a new perspective on

比如说：

  • 你喜欢看书么
    • 看新闻；看报纸；看景点
  • 学生应不应该评判老师（老师看到了学生的反馈）
  • 历史重不重要
  • 在别的国家工作会不会对这个社会有新的看法

5. 底层逻辑：放松1

  • 什么时候放松
    • when I feel stressed (out) and anxious, overwhelmed by
  • 一个令我放松的事情
    • an escape from the stressful life
  • 形容一下令我放松的事情
    • therapeutic 有助于精神放松的
    • recharge my batteries 给自己充电
    • loosen up in my mind and body 身心放松
  • 什么能放松
    • perform aerobic exercise
    • entertainment facilities
    • shopping mall

​

6. 底层逻辑：放松2

什么时候放松？ 累的时候，累有两种：心累，身累

  • 心累
    • stressed and anxious
  • 身累
    • drained and exhausted

什么导致累？

  • be swamp with
  • tend to do / be inclined to do

形容放松的事情

  • therapeutic and refreshing
  • which can elevate my spirits 振奋精神
  • be engrossed in 全神贯注
  • leave those worries behind
  • declutter your mind 整理我的思绪
  • it is a great pastime
  • spice up my routine life 让我的日常生活更有趣

7. 底层逻辑：放松3

什么时候放松？

  • worn out 筋疲力竭

什么样的事去放松？

  • a marvelous form of recreation

形容一下这个事情

  • create an agreeable atmosphere where I can find my inner peace and spiritual harmony
    • agreeable 可以换成 amazing, pleasant
    • atmosphere 可以换成 ambience
  • it can feel the void inside me
  • help me escape from the hustle and bustle of city life
    • get away from my work for a spell

8. Part3：正反两方面

  • to be honest, I have mixed feelings about …
  • ambivament 喜忧参半的
    • to be honest, I am ambivalent about it
  • it just like a double-edged sword
    • double-edged sword
  • dialectical attitude 辩证的态度

9. 底层逻辑：政府(上)

为什么政府应该做？

  • segment
    • a large segment of government revenues comes from taxes paid by citizens
  • be obliged to de
  • incumbent on 在职者
    • it is incumbent on somebody to do something 去做某事是某人的职责
  • be attentive to 考虑到；照顾到
  • establish some rules and regulations
    • to approach the problem

万能解决方法:

  • they can launch some education campaigns and outreach activities to preach the importance of …
  • early detection and timely intervention can remedy the situation instead of using some empty slogans
    • remedy 挽回，挽救

10. 底层逻辑：政府(下)

为什么不做？

  • the limited fiscal budget
  • considering ~
  • the government should allocate their resources and funds more reasonably
  • if they devote too much to this particular domain, other equally important fields may be underdeveloped
    • such as science research, infrastructure development, pension system
  • retard the comprehensive development of the society
    • which may ~
    • retard n.智障；v. 阻碍
    • so it is incumbent on them to strike a balance between the spendings on each field
  • 政府需要 see a big picture 顾全大局
  • analyse the situation from the holistic perspective 从整体的角度

11. 万能开头

12. “有助于”和“不利于”的表达

  • be conducive to

    • be conducive to my health
    • be conducive to my career prospects
    • be conducive to the economy
    • be conducive to the development

  • be bad for

  • be harmful to

  • be detrimental to

  • jeopardize

13. 底层逻辑：心理压力

为什么压力大？因为我们都很忙

  • busy
  • lead a hectic existence 可以代替 busy
    • I lead a hectic existence

具体怎么忙？

  • I have to reconcile A with B
    • I have to reconcile my career prospects with my family commitments. You know, it is draining and demanding to strike a balance between them.
    • reconcile 兼顾
  • people may be under enormous psychological and mental pressure, so you can hardly spare time to do what you are passionate about.

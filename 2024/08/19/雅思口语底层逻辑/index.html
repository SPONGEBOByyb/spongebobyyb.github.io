雅思口语底层逻辑（小红书）

1. 口语开场废话

  • 重复题目
    • Speaking of …
    • When it comes to …
    • Regarding/Concerning …
  • 引出话题
    • What comes/floats into my mind first is …
    • The first thing that springs to my head is
    • off the top of my head

2. Work/Study

  • I have been nursing a desire to be a + 职业
    • nurse 动词：怀揣
  • It is my true vocation 我天生就是干这个事的人
  • and not just bread and butter
    • bread and butter 此处表示谋生手段
  • It is a job or a major that can stretch me and fulfil my selfworth
    • stretch 本意：拉伸，延展；竭尽所能，发挥全部本领
  • when i do something, i can feel a sense of achievement and recognition
  • that’s just like a motivator to me to go further in this domain

3. hometown

my hometown is …

located in … (the northeast of China 哈尔滨)

  • it is a metropolis/cosmopolis full of skyscrapers and advanced infrastructure
    • metropolis / cosmopolis
    • skyscraper
    • infrastructure

你的家乡有什么，infrastructure, such as hospital, shopping mall, subway

  • they are ubiquitous on the street
    • ubiquitous 无处不在的

城市除了基础设施，还有特点，景色，以…闻名

  • renowed
    • be renowed for various tourist attractions

然后介绍这个城市真实的旅游景点，总之，这个地方非常的迷人

  • captivating
  • unique
  • every vacation, it appeals to tourists from every corner of the Globe
    • from every corner of the Globe 来自全球各地

总而言之，一提到我的家乡就有自豪感和归属感

  • whenever mentioning my hometown, I can feel a sense of belonging and pride.

4. 底层逻辑：我不会

  • 原因
    • beyond my grasp
    • be clumsy in
    • aptitude
  • 事物本身
    • complicated
    • inaccessible
    • obscure
    • elusive
  • difficult 替换为 demanding
  • 感受
    • overwhelmed and inadequate

5. 底层逻辑：放松1

  • 什么时候放松
    • when I feel stressed (out) and anxious, overwhelmed by
  • 一个令我放松的事情
    • an escape from the stressful life
  • 形容一下令我放松的事情
    • therapeutic 有助于精神放松的
    • recharge my batteries 给自己充电
    • loosen up in my mind and body 身心放松
  • 什么能放松
    • perform aerobic exercise
    • entertainment facilities
    • shopping mall

​

6. 底层逻辑：放松2

什么时候放松？ 累的时候，累有两种：心累，身累

  • 心累
    • stressed and anxious
  • 身累
    • drained and exhausted

什么导致累？

  • be swamped with
  • tend to do / be inclined to do

形容放松的事情

  • therapeutic and refreshing
  • which can elevate my spirits 振奋精神
  • be engrossed in 全神贯注
  • leave those worries behind
  • declutter your mind 整理我的思绪
  • it is a great pastime
  • spice up my routine life 让我的日常生活更有趣

7. 底层逻辑：放松3

什么时候放松？

  • worn out 筋疲力竭

什么样的事去放松？

  • a marvelous form of recreation

形容一下这个事情

  • create an agreeable atmosphere where I can find my inner peace and spiritual harmony
    • agreeable 可以换成 amazing, pleasant
    • atmosphere 可以换成 ambience
  • it can fill the void inside me
  • help me escape from the hustle and bustle of city life
    • get away from my work for a spell

8. 底层逻辑：broaden my horizons(看)

  • have access to
  • digest whatever aspects I can turn into benefit
  • good way to expand my boundary of knowledge and experience
  • have a new perspective on

比如说：

  • 你喜欢看书么
    • yes, I like reading book cos you know, I can have accesss to those latest information and those useful resoureces. you know, and I can digest whatever aspects I can turn into benefit. you know, that’s really good way to expand my boundary of knowledge and experience, so that I can have a new perspective on this world.
    • 看新闻；看报纸；看景点
    • yes I like visiting other countries cos you know, I can have access to those tourist attactions and these local specialties and their custom their culture, I can digest whatever aspects I can turn into benefit. you know, that’s a really good way to expand my boundary of knowledge and experience, so that I can have a new perspective on this country.
  • 学生应不应该评判老师（老师看到了学生的反馈）
  • 历史重不重要
  • 在别的国家工作会不会对这个社会有新的看法

9. 底层逻辑：看

broaden my horizons

“看”的表达：

  • be exposed to
  • have access to

能看到什么：

  • his logic
  • his attitude
  • his theory
  • his thinking patterns

不管看到什么，都要把有用的知识吸收进来

  • assimilate whatever aspects i can turn into benefit
  • so it’s really perfect way to open up new avenues of thought
    • open up new avenues of thought
  • so that it can advance my understanding of something

10. 底层逻辑：政府(上)

为什么政府应该做？

  • segment
    • a large segment of government revenues comes from taxes paid by citizens
  • be obliged to do
  • incumbent on 在职者
    • it is incumbent on somebody to do something 去做某事是某人的职责
  • be attentive to 考虑到；照顾到
  • establish some rules and regulations
    • to approach the problem

万能解决方法:

  • they can launch some education campaigns and outreach activities to preach the importance of …
  • early detection and timely intervention can remedy the situation instead of using some empty slogans
    • remedy 挽回，挽救

11. 底层逻辑：政府(下)

为什么不做？

  • the limited fiscal budget
  • considering ~
  • the government should allocate their resources and funds more reasonably
  • if they devote too much to this particular domain, other equally important fields may be underdeveloped
    • such as science research, infrastructure development, pension system
  • retard the comprehensive development of the society
    • which may ~
    • retard n.智障；v. 阻碍
    • so it is incumbent on them to strike a balance between the spendings on each field
  • 政府需要 see a big picture 顾全大局
  • analyse the situation from the holistic perspective 从整体的角度

12. 底层逻辑：心理压力

为什么压力大？因为我们都很忙

  • busy
  • lead a hectic existence 可以代替 busy
    • I lead a hectic existence

具体怎么忙？

  • I have to reconcile A with B
    • I have to reconcile my career prospects with my family commitments. You know, it is draining and demanding to strike a balance between them.
    • reconcile 兼顾
  • people may be under enormous psychological and mental pressure, so you can hardly spare time to do what you are passionate about.

13. 底层逻辑：convenience

is really convenient 的同境词

  • easily available

既然这么方便，那就可以帮助我们

  • have access to do something
  • with less effort and difficulty
  • bring us much convenience and flexibility
  • make good use of our time energy / use our time and energy constructively
  • 从而 with better efficiency and productivity

总而言之，这个方便的东西

  • provide a seamless and pleasant experience

14. 底层逻辑：上进1

improve myself 大家都会

  • ceaselessly
    • improve myself ceaselessly

提升自己的过程为了

  • idendify areas where I need to overcome my deficiencies and improve my inadequacies
    • deficiencies 可以替换成 shortcoming
  • surpass myself 超越自我
  • move up another notch 更上一层；更进一步

15. 底层逻辑：上进2

首先，想上进的话，就要不断提升自己，不断去学习知识：

  • expand your repertoire of abilities and skills 扩大技能库

扩大技能库是为了：

  • sharpen my competitive edge
    • edge 优势
  • separate yourself from others
  • gain a foothold in …

第一题：why do you study English? (Part1)

yes cos you know in the modern society, English is so important, a lot of companies requires your English abilities or some certificates, so I gotta study English to expand my repertoire of ability and skills to sharpen my competitive edge. I believe you know this language can help me separate myself from others in my workplace and help me gain a foothold in society.

第二题：介绍一个将来你要学的东西 (part2)

第三题：do you think language learning is important? (part3)

第四题：为什么现在社会这么卷呢？

cos you know in this society if you want to separate yourself from others, if you want to gain a foothold in your workplace, if you want to earn better salaries, you have to improve yourself constantly, you have to expand your repertoire of abilities and skills to sharpen your competitive edge. So every day this has become more ambitious, this has become more diligent, and rat race is a foregone conclusion.

  • rat race 内卷
  • foregone conclusion 必然发生的事

16. 底层逻辑：like

针对part3部分以及写作

一个东西的出现和存在，一定是专门针对一个群体的，不可能所有人都喜欢它，所以这个东西一定是：

  • oriented towards (on particular group)
  • be tailored for sb 为某人量身定做
    • 为什么这个东西年轻人喜欢 ，因为 be tailored for young people

熟悉，熟知，除了 be familiar with ,还有

  • be well acquainted with
    • the designer must be well acquainted with their targeted customers preferences hobbies and personalities

所以这个产品才能做到

  • kindle their interests 激起某人的兴趣
  • spark their curiosity 勾起某人的好奇心
  • whet their appetite to explore more 勾起某人的欲望去做某事

喜欢，的一个非常好的表达

  • gravitate towards sth 受某事吸引

17. 底层逻辑：描述任何聚会，节日

无论大小聚会，都要

  • gather together
  • enjoy each other’s company 享受彼此的陪伴

之后

  • it is a great bonding experience 是一个非常好的加深感情的经历

聚会都干什么，吃喝 …

  • eat barbecue, drink some beer
  • 过春节吃饺子，圣诞节吃火鸡

除了吃喝，还有聊天（两个collocation）

  • shoot the breeze 吹牛侃大山；朋友之间闲聊
  • share some anecdotes

描述一下这个氛围，除了atmosphere

  • ambience
  • 氛围是怎样的？
    • vibrant 充满活力的
    • electrifying 令人兴奋激动的
    • cordial 温暖宜人的
    • it is bustling with joy and excitement and laughter 充满着欢声笑语

18. 底层逻辑：描述所有看过的电影，书籍等

描述故事的两个单词

  • thrilling
  • electrifying

然后，它一定是

  • be full of twists and turns and cliffhangers
    • twists and turns 表示峰回路转
    • cliffhangers 表示悬念
  • just like rollercoaster
    • rollercoaster 过山车
  • which can keep me in suspense 让我的心一直悬着
    • suspence 也表示悬念

接下来，

  • as the story unfolded my first guess turned out to be totally wrong
  • at the end of the story, i was astonished / i was shocked
  • and i really enjoyed this sensation
    • sensation 感觉

最后扣题

  • so that’s why i really like this movie / book.

19. 底层逻辑：情绪价值

一段经历能给你情绪价值，它一定是：

  • rewarding experience

我们的感受：

  • I can feel a sense of achievement and self-worth

这种感觉能够：

  • boost my confidence and self-esteem

然后可以：

  • good for my personal development and growth
  • 或者 good for my mental health

20. 底层逻辑：人物

两个形容词

  • sophisticated 精于世故的
  • charismatic 有魅力的

然后

  • just like the synonym of excellence 优秀的代言
  • he literally has all essential qualities of …
  • so that’s why he can separate himself from others 脱颖而出

最后学一个最好的表达

  • worth emulation 值得效仿；值得我们学习
    • his spirit / his courage is worth our emulation

21. 底层逻辑：社会

无论什么样的问题，无非就是对社会好处和坏处

好处的进阶思路：

  • it just like a virtuous circle
    • virtuous circle 良性循环
  • which can improve the public’s well-being and contribute social stability and cohesion

不好的：

  • 恶性循环：vicious circle
  • which can cause discontentment and indignation among the society 导致社会的不满和愤懑不平

最终会

  • result in social instability and turmoil
    • turmoil 混乱

22. 底层逻辑：性格（坚韧的）

两个表达(必须两个一起用）：

  • resilient
  • tenacious

he is so resilient and tenacious

  • i’m inspired by his indomitable spirit
    • indomitable 永不言败的；永不言弃的
  • encounter some challenges and adversities 遇到困难和逆境
  • he can compose himself 让自己冷静下来
    • composed 冷静的
  • embrace them 拥抱挑战和困境
  • persisted until he succeeded 坚持直到成功

而我认为这种精神：

  • inspiring and motivational
  • worth our emulation 值得大家效仿

23. 底层逻辑：体育，竞争

为什么喜欢体育？

  • just like a roller coaster

尤其到了比赛的关键时刻

  • especially in the clutch time
    • clutch time 关键时刻

两支球队不相上下，难舍难分

  • both teams are evenly matched
    • evenly matched 难解难分

所以

  • a momentary lapse of concentration can be crucial
    • momentary 短暂的
    • lapse of concentration 注意力不集中
  • there is no margin for error
    • margin for error 犯错的余地
  • there is no room for sentiment
  • if you want to win, if you want to defeat your opponent ,play / struggle at full strength
    • at full strength 全力

24. 底层逻辑：描述朋友和家人

  • reliable and trustworthy
  • and just like my solid backup force

当我们

  • have a mood swing

朋友就是

  • somebody that I can confide in
  • somebody is in my confidence

所以我就可以

  • turn to him for solace 寻求安慰
  • unburden myself to
  • to relieve my emotional baggage 放下情绪包袱

28. Part2必用

什么时候？看的书，看的电影

开头：

  • approximately
    • let us wind the clock back approximately + 一段时间

that was a really rewarding/fruitful experience

    • salutary lesson 有益的教训

      • 可以把experience换成episode
  • which was incredibly imprinted in my mind and I really cherish it

29. 底层逻辑：兴趣（3个collocation）

  • align with (符合)
    • align with my interest
  • spark my curiosity（激起）
  • whet my appetite for （激起我的欲望和兴趣）

实例：喜欢一本书
the content/the theme/the gist align with my some interest and the scenario/those stories can spark my curiosity and whet my appetite for exploring the magical world.

实例：科学
I like science.it makes me light.i mean this subject really aligns with my interest and spark my curiosity ，you know，i can have access to a lot of practical examples and experiments which can whet my appetite to find out more of it

实例：媒体话题、广告
useful and practical information which can align with the customers interests，and spark their curiosity and whet their appetite to make a purchase

实例：大数据，推送
according to your browsing history the big data can provide some content which can align with your interest and and spark my curiosity to whet your appetite for watching

30. 替换know, understand, important, really

  • know = be clued up about
    • I know how to do this
    • I am clued up about how to do this
  • understand = grasp
    • 例一
      • I can understand the meaning of your words
      • I can grasp the meaning of your words
    • 例二
      • I don’t understand what you mean
      • I don’t grasp what you mean
  • important = indispensable
    • 例一
      • English is important in today’s society
      • English is indispensable in today’s society
    • 例二
      • Money is important
      • Money is indispensable
  • really = genuinely
    • 例一
      • I’m really interested in this book
      • I’m genuinely interested in this book
    • 例二
      • I really like your style
      • I genuinely like your style

31. 必备的一句话

the three dimensional image and advanced stereo system can offer superb sensory experience, which can satisfy your desire for visual and auditory stimulation.

  • three dimensional 3D画面
  • advanced stereo system 高级的音响设备
  • superb sensory experience 非常好的感官体验
  • 涉及视频的话可以将“three dimensional image 3D画面”换成“dynamic image”; “advanced stereo system 高级音响系统”换成“sound effect”

well I think it’s difficult to generalize cos it varies from person to person. I think it’s just a matter of individual preference. but if I have to speculate.

  • difficult to generalize
  • it varies from person to person
  • just a matter of individual preference
  • But if I have to speculate, probably

33. 底层逻辑：景色的描述

首先，可以欣赏到好的景色：

欣赏，除了appreciate或者enjoy,

  • feast my eyes on sth 让我大饱眼福（让我的眼睛享用这个景色）

景色可以用：

  • natural landscape 自然风光

非常好的景色，“好”的表达：

  • amazing, picturesque, breathtaking

连在一起：I can feast my eyes on this amazing natural landscape.

不管描述什么地方，一定有：

  • waterfront 海滨，水边
    • stunning waterfront

可以把“有”，换成：

  • pride itself on … 以此为傲

所以：this place prides itself on a stunning waterfront.

接下来就是描述waterfront：

  • sparking waters 波光粼粼的水面
  • radiant sunshine 灿烂的阳光

然后，这两个东西：

  • just creat picturespue backdrop
    • backdrop 表示“背景”的意思
  • which is ideal for photography, picnics and leisurely strolls

最后，把所有的连在一起：

yes, I like this place. I can feast my eyes on its amazing natural landscape. you know, it just pride itself on a stunning waterfront, you know, sparking waters and radiant sunchine just creat picturesque backdrop, which is ideal for photography, picnics and leisurely strolls. yeah, I like this place you know, when I immerse myself in this ambience, I can leave those worries behind. I can find my inner peace and spiritual harmony that is really therapeutic, and I think that’s a perfect way to unwind properly. So that’s why I like to stay there.

1. Breakfast

Do you have the same routine every morning?

Yes, absolutely. I start with some exercises, then a cup of tea.

This can keep my mind calm and make my body more healthy.

Do you think breakfast is important?

Yes, I think it is crucial for me.

Because it can make me refreshing

and after breakfast I can be full of energy to deal with my business efficiently.

Was your morning routine different when you were younger?

Not really. I always lead a hectic existence.

Now I also need to reconcile my homework with some examination,

so I eat breakfast quickly and start my busy day both as a children and now.

Is there anything you’d change about your mornings now?

Not really. I think now my mornings is full, and I like this feeling.

maybe, I have been familiar with this life style.

one busy start can make me have a fruitful day.

2. Exciting Activity

What did you find exciting as a child?

When I was a child, I found playing football is exciting for me.

I always played it with my friends.

when I was stressful and anxious it can loosed up in my mind and body.

Do you think sports like climbing are exciting? （放松）

Yes, definitely. I believe climbing is a very exciting sport, full of challenges.

I can have access to various landscapes at different altitudes.

It is a good way to expand my boundary of experience,

(and I can have new perspectives on this world.)

Would you prefer an exciting or relaxing vacation?（放松）

I prefer to exciting vacation.

cos I think my daily life is stressful and boring, so I hope my vacation can be more exciting.

Exciting vacation is therapeutic and refreshing, it can declutter my mind and spice up my routine life.

Have you done anything exciting recently?（体育，竞争）

yeah, definitely!

last week, I played a tennis match with my friends.

this match was just like roller coaster, especially in the clutch time.

We are evenly matched, so there is no margin for error and there is no room for sentiment.

that’s why I think it’s exciting for me.

3. Internet

When did you start using the internet？

I remember when I was seven years old, I have access to the internet.

I thought it’s novel and interesting,

and on weekends I could watch some cartoons online.

How often do you go online?

almost every day. sometime I can do some research and study online,

and I also watch some interesting movies or play video games to relax myself.

I think the internet is essential in my life.

Can you remember a time you weren’t allow to use the internet?

Yes, definitely. I clearly remember

when I was thirteen years old, before the final examination, I didn’t review any knowledge and played video games online approximately one hour.

I was scolded by my parents for these things, then wasn’t allowed to use the internet.

What would you do without the internet?

do some sports like playing football and tennis to spice up my routine life.

I am a big fan of tennis.

I can also read some book to expand my boundary of knowledge and skills.

15. Bikes

Did you have a bike when you were a child?

Yes, I had a bike when I was a kid, and it was one of my favorite toys.

I used to ride it almost every day after school, and it gave me a great sense of freedom and fun.

Do you have a bike now?

Yes, I have a bike now. I seldom ride it, but it is my father’s mean of commuting

as my father thinks cycling is a healthy lifestyle meanwhile a time-saving way to commute in busy peaktime.

Do you often ride a bike now?

No, I don’t ride a bike much anymore,

i ride a bike only when i have plenty of time and truly like to go out and enjoy the natural world with my friends.

but most of time, i would rather take electric bike. because it is much faster than bikes and as for long distance, i could save much time and energy.

(Although it’s convenient to get around with a bike, I usually choose to ride my electric bike..

because it is slower than my electric vehicle.)

Do you think bikes are popular in your country？

Yes, absolutely! Nowadays, more and more people tend to lead a healthy lifestyle,

so they often go out by bike or in their free time, they can ride a bike to exercise their muscles.

(An increasing number of individuals are inclined to adopt a healthy lifestyle.

Consequently, they frequently opt for cycling as a means of transportation or engage in biking during their leisure time)

16. Coins

Do you use coins now?

No, I hardly use coins now. (Because of their inconvenience, )

most of the time, I prefer using mobile payments or cards, (which it is way more convenient.)

（括号里的句子二选一）

Is it convenient to use coins?

No, I don’t think so.（To be honest, I don’t find using coins convenient.）Because They are easy to lose and difficult to carry around.

(But electronic payments are the opposite, in most situation, they are faster and more convenient.

So I rarely opt for coins.)

Did you collect coins when you were a kid?

Yes, when I was a child, I collected a variety of coins.

I had a cute piggy bank at home, and whenever I had coins in my hands, I would put them in!

Because I didn’t have too much pocket money, when I wanted to buy something beyond my means, I can use the coins in the piggy bank.

Do you carry coins when you go out?

No, I seldom carry coins when I go out these days.

As I mentioned before, because coins are easy to lose.

Most of the time, I prefer to carry my phone (so that I can pay through Alipay or Wechat Pay, )

which makes payments easier.

18. Streets

Are the streets in the area where you live busy?

Yes, I think so. the streets in my area are really busy, especially during rush hours.

because it is a residential area, there are lots of young people who need to go to school or work at almost the same time.

So traffic congestion is not inevitable, and making it take longer to get anywhere.

How do people cross the streets in the city where you live?

They may take public transports such as bus or subway.

If their destination is close to their home, they can cross the streets by bike or on foot.

What is the condition of the streets in your city?

I believe the streets in my city are in pretty good condition.

The roads are well-maintained and clean, with most of the main streets being wide and easy to drive on.

Do you think the streets in your city need improvement?

To be honest, I am quite satisfied with the streets in my city.

overall, they are pretty good, most roads are well- maintained.

I think they only need some minor repairs regularly.

19. Laughter

What do you think laughter means to daily life?

I think laughter is a symbol of happiness,

and people who always laugh should fairly satisfy their daily life.

Maybe sometimes some anecdotes in daily life can make us laught.

When was the last time you laughed?

I remember, it was last night.

When I browsed Tiktok videos, I watched a really funny one.

Although the plot was so simple, the exaggerated performances made me laugh out loud.

Do you think you can make other people laugh?

Absolutely yes, I believe I am a humorous people,

I am a big fan of funny talk shows, so I have so many interesting jokes in my mind.

At some parties, those jokes can always bring people joy and laughter .

Do you like to watch funny movies?

Yes,definitely. I do like watch funny movies.

when I am stressed and drained, overwhelmed by my hard homework and examination,

I am willing to watch a funny movies to spice up my routine life.

I think it is a good way to help me escape from my stressful life.

Do you always laught at funny things?

my bar is low

20. Fishing

Do you like eating fish?

Absolutely yes, fish is one of my favorite foods.

It is not only very yummy, and it is also full of nutrients and proteins.

That’s why I do like eating fish.

Did you go fishing?

Yes, I remember When I was a child, I went fishing with my family.

Because I was so young, and I didn’t know some fishing skills.

I waited for almost half day, but I have no fish, so I was so frustrating.

Have you been to the fish market?

Yes, I have. Because I am a big fan of eating fish, I always go to the fish market with my mother.

The fish market has strong, special scents, but the dishes made of fish is so fresh and delicious.

Is fishing popular in your country?

Yes, I think so.

Because of the increasing number of aged population in my country, these elderly people tend to choose a peaceful activity to kill their time.

So most of them can go fishing, and sometimes this activity can make them feel a sense of achievement.

21. Teacher

Do you have a favorite teacher?

Yes, I have. my chemical teacher in my high school is my favorite teacher.

he is pretty humorous and he explain some theories clearly.

So I could get a good grade in chemistry.

Are you still in touch with your primary school teacher?

To be honest, I aren’t in touch with my primary school teachers.

I remember three years ago, I go back to my primary school to see my teachers,

But it is regretful, most of them have retired a long time ago.

In what way did your favorite teacher help you?

Because of some hard issues I could not solve it, or the answer I didn’t understand,

I always went to ask my chemical teacher for help.

He did not stop until I fully understood.

Do you want to be a teacher in the future?

To be honest, I don’t want to be a teacher in the future.

I think a teacher must be patient and considerate.

But I can’t do it, and i cannot stand doing something repetitive and tedious every day .

22. Perfume

Do you like perfume?

It depends on its scent.

I like some mild perfumes, don’t like those strong perfumes.

Do you use perfume in your daily life?

No, I don’t use perfume in my daily life.

But if I have an appointment with somebody important, maybe I will use it.

Where do you spray perfume?

I typically/usually apply perfume to my wrists or the sides of my neck.

This way, the scent isn’t too strong but still lasts throughout the day.

Will you buy perfume as a gift?

I think it depends on the recipient’s gender.

If the recipient is female, I may consider buying perfume as a gift.

But if I buy it for male, I feel that it may not be suitable.

Do you think perfumes are expensive?

It depends. I believe perfumes prices vary a lot.

Some brands are reasonably priced,

and if you choose a scent that suits you, the value is worth it.

23. Make friends

Do you like to make friends?

Yes, definitely. I do like to make friends.

When I meet some difficulties, they can help me to solve it.

Have you made many friends when you were a child?

Yes, I have.

When I was a child, I had lots of friends.

after school, we always played together, and because of them, I had a happy childhood.

Have you made any new friends recently?

To be honest, I have not made any new friends recently.

Because these days I am so busy, and I have to reconcile my homework and examination.

I can hardly spare my time to make new friends.

What do you talk about with your friends?

Maybe we can share some anecdotes or shoot the breeze,

it is bustling with joy and excitement and laughter.

I think it a good way to escape from my routine life.

Did you like to talk with others when you were a child?

No, when I was a child, I am pretty introverted.

I prefered to enjoy myself and seldom talked with others.

24. Teamwork

Do you like teamwork?

Yes, I do enjoy teamwork.

I think it is very productive,

and I can gain different perspectives from my teammates.

Have you teamed up with someone else before?

Yes, I have teamed up with others before.

In my major projects, teamwork is often essential for successful completion.

For instance, in my recent project, I collaborated with my classmates to achieve our goals effectively.

What do you learn from working in a team?

As I mentioned before, I can gain different perspectives from my teammates.

I ‘ve also improve my communication skills, learning how to express my thoughts clearly and listen actively to others.

What do you dislike about teamwork?

Although my teammates have lots of ideas, we can only choose one to apply.

So we always spend more time discussing.

I think it’s not efficient and I dislike.

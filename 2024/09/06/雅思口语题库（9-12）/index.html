49-有趣的著名城市

Well, I’d like to talk about a famous city that I find interesting, I think it is Chengdu actually, the first time I know there about a couple of years ago if I am not mistaken. Because my roommate’s hometown is Chengdu, and he always introduce some interesting places in Chengdu to us.

Well, I’d say it is quite famous for two reason. The first reason is Chendu has delicous hotpot. For example, when I went there for the first time, I went to several famous hotpot restaurant to try their hotpot. I am telling you, the hotpot in Chengdu is the best because it seems that I have never had before, and Every time I travel there, I would happily ear hotpot for seven days a week！（You know what? _ . It’s fair to say that if she hadn’t _______, she wouldn’t have _______.）

Another reason I’d like to mention is Mahjong culture. I mean Mahjong culture is also an interesting aspect of Chengdu. To be specific, it is well known that Chengdu people, both young and old, love to play mahjong. The last time I was in Chengdu, I saw there were all sorts of mahjong club in the neighborhood, where old people would gather to play mahjong. Besides, I heard that young people will also play mahjong mobile games in any of their free time. It’s an interesting culture that we can’t find anywhere else.

Besides, I find the Sichuan dialect very interesting. The way they pronounce and express themselves is like telling jokes, which often makes me laugh.

So, I’d say Chengdu is a vibrant city, full of interesting things and delicious food.
